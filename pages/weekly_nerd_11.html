Weekly Nerd - Guido Bouman

I'm an interaction engineer specialising in crafting simple, elegant & intuitive solutions to complex problems. My specialties: Front-end web development (new concepts, interactions, architecture setup & performance optimisation) and interaction design (recreating physical interactions in the digital world). Working as interaction engineer on various projects for companies like Design Museum London, Rijksmuseum, Van Gogh Museum, Staatsloterij and Malmberg.

You can hack a coffee machine! Guido talked about the spot where he works (Q42), how to work with IoT devices and hack them and how to setup a good IoT project from scratch.
